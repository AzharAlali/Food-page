Recipe for Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of whole grain white rice
  • 2 pound of mixed veggies or freshly cut carrot, green peas, sweet corn, green beans
  • 2 Whole onions
  • Salt
  • Nature seasoning or chicken knorr
  • red crushed pepper or cayenne pepper
  • thyme leaves
  • curry powder
  • Any meat of your choice (I would be using chicken in this recipe)
  • Vegetable oil or olive oil or any other of your choice

Instructions

  1. Boil chicken with spices of choice to done or nearly done
  2. Grill chicken on each side for 8 - 10 minutes on 400 degree fahrenheits
  3. Put water in a pot and add salt, Nature seasoning, red crushed pepper and curry and bring to a boil
  4. While water is boiling, thorougly wash rice about 6 - 8 times to get rid of starch
  5. Once water has reached a boil, add rice to boiling water and constantly check till it is soft or to desired textured
  6. In a large sauce pan add about 1/4 cups of oil and allow to heat up for about 2 minutes
  7. Add frozen veggies or fresh veggies and spices and allow to stir fry for about 2 to 2 minnutes but not too long
  8. Gradually add cooked rice to sauce pan, and turn and fold untill all Ingredients are mixed
  9. Serve with chicken on plate and enjoy
